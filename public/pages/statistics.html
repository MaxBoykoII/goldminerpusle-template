Production Based on Last/Latest Four Quarters

Using the most recently available quarterly production reports, the gold and silver stocks listed on the TSX/TSXv are reporting production totals on a rolling 4 quarters of:

  • Gold: 25.17M ounces(862.97 tons or 782.87 tonnes), a -0.4% change over the 4 quarters ending at the prior quarter.
  • Silver: 179.02M ounces(6,137.83 tons or 5,568.14 tonnes), a -3.2% change over the previous 4 quarters ending at the prior quarter.
